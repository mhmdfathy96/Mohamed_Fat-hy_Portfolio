# Sales & Closing System

## Discovery Call Structure (30 Minutes)

1. Founder Context (5 min)
   - What are you building?
   - Current stage?
   - Timeline expectations?

2. Problem Clarification (10 min)
   - What is blocking you?
   - Technical uncertainty?
   - Budget clarity?

3. Solution Framing (10 min)
   - Outline recommended phase
   - Timeline expectation
   - Architecture approach

4. Qualification (5 min)
   - Budget range confirmation
   - Decision-making authority
   - Next step agreement

---

## After Call Process

Within 48 hours:
- Send structured proposal
- Include scope, timeline, milestones
- Include payment schedule

---

## Payment Model (Recommended)

Milestone-based:

- 30% upfront
- 40% mid-development
- 30% before launch

---

## Proposal Structure

1. Executive Summary
2. Scope Breakdown
3. Timeline
4. Milestones
5. Investment
6. Assumptions & Exclusions
7. Next Steps
